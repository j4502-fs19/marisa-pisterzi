Get to know me

I am a junior Convergence Journalism and Political Science major at the University of Missouri.

In college, I have worked as a digital producer for KOMU 8 News, the NBC affiliate station in Columbia, Missouri. I have also reported on the Missouri State Capitol for the Columbia Missourian earlier in 2019. In high school, I had my first journalism gig as the Editor-in-Chief for my school’s newspaper, BluePrints.

Throughout my career at Mizzou, I’ve had the opportunity to work on a documentary about AIDS activist and reality tv icon, Pedro Zamora. I traveled to San Fransisco to interview cast mates from MTV’s: The Real World San Fransisco where I discovered my passion for documentaries and the entertainment industry.

Outside of journalism, I have a love for live music, homemade gnocchi and stand-up comedy.
